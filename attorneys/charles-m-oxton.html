Chip Oxton

Charles “Chip” Oxton is a Santa Barbara attorney representing clients in all aspects of family law and civil litigation.

Chip is a highly-skilled and respected legal advisor and litigator with over 48 years of experience practicing law in Santa Barbara. He has represented clients in a broad range of legal matters, including divorce and custody cases, personal injury and civil disputes, conservatorship and guardianship, wills and probate administration, real estate law, and landlord-tenant law.

Legal disputes are often intense, emotionally-charged experiences for the parties involved. Chip’s approach to practicing law involves providing clients with calm, reasoned advice in an environment devoted to constructive problem-solving. Chip combines a wealth of knowledge, experience, and litigation expertise with diligent and exhaustive preparation, to provide his clients with high quality legal representation.

Chip’s practice is focused in and around Santa Barbara County.
